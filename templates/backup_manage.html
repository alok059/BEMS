💡 Backup Tips

✅ Before Major Changes

Always create a manual backup before bulk import, deletion, or system updates.

📤 Download Important Backups

Download backups to your computer or cloud storage for extra safety.

⚠️ Restore Carefully

Restoring replaces current data. A safety backup is created automatically before restore.

📅 Regular Schedule

Automatic backups run daily at 2 AM. Ensure your computer is running at that time.

🔍 Verify Backups

Periodically download and test a backup to ensure it's working properly.

🗂️ Naming Convention

Backups are named with timestamp: bems_backup_20241225_020000.db (Dec 25, 2:00 AM)
